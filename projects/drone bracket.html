Class Project: Drone Bracket

Skills: [SolidWorks, SolidWorks Simulation, 3D Printing]

This was a project for my SolidWorks simulation course at WSU, ME 216. The purpose of the assignment was to design a 3D printed bracket for a package carrying drone. The first step was to determine the material properties of the different infill percentages that were available to us and put them into SolidWorks as custom materials. The load we would use in the simulation was then calculated from the expected load and the factor of safety. Finally, SolidWorks topology optimization was used to determine the most mass efficient design. The final design was based off of these results and tested in a different simulation study to confirm it met the requirements.
