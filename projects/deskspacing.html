DeskSpacing.com

I created the website DeskSpacing.com with a friend over this past summer to help people plan out their desk setups. It got a lot of traction early on, with 40,000 page visits in the first three days because of a Reddit post and is still holding steady at an average of 40 visits per day. I made all of the 3D models in Blender and wrote the Javascript for the site functionality. Although this project isn't directly related to engineering, I still consider it the most impressive thing I've built because to make it I had to learn Blender and Javascript on my own in a short amount of time, two things I had never worked with before.
