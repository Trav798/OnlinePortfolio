Class Project: Waste Paper Towel Sorting

Skills: [Arduino, Rhino, Design Engineering]

The final project for my Intro to Engineering Class was to work in teams of four to develop a way of separating paper towels from other waste in a restroom. The project was sponsored by Kimberly Clark, and our solutions were judged by representatives from the company. Our solution was essentially an attachment to the garbage bins already in Mackey that would only open for a brief period after someone had taken a paper towel from the dispenser. I 3D modeled the final version of our solution for our presentation in Rhino and developed a method for a prototype to be manufactured using laser cut panels. At the end of the class, we turned in a design report which summarized our methodology and analyzed how well we met the criteria and constraints of the problem.
